The Himalayas are a popular destination for trekkers and mountaineers, with Mount Everest being the most sought-after peak to climb. The Himalayas are also an important source of fresh water for many countries, with major rivers such as the Ganges, Indus, and Brahmaputra originating in the mountain range. The Himalayas are also known for their rich cultural heritage, with many ancient temples, monasteries, and shrines located in the region. The Himalayas are also known for their beautiful landscapes and picturesque views, with many trekking routes and scenic drives that offer panoramic views of the mountain range.
